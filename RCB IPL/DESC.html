SUNRISERS HYDERABAD

Sunrisers Hyderabad are giving it all they've got in the Indian Premier League 2024. Out of the eleven editions that they have featured in, they have made it to the playoffs six times. They also lifted the trophy in 2016. This year, with some of the most exciting players joining the team, they are geared up for a power-packed season.Ready to rally behind the #OrangeArmy? Stay tuned for ticket and match updates of SRH!

Sunrisers Hyderabad TATA IPL 2024 Season Preview

Clad in new colours and captained by a world cup winner in Pat Cummins,
the Sunrisers Hyderabad begin their TATA IPL 2024 campaign against the Kolkata Knight Riders on March 23rd at the Eden Gardens.
With a squad brimming with talent - both international and domestic, the Sunrisers will be coached by Daniel Vettori for the 2024 campaign.
The #OrangeArmy will surely be looking forward to the return of players - we are too!


TATA IPL 2024 - Sunrisers Hyderabad Auction Overview

At the TATA IPL 2024 auction, Sunrisers Hyderabad made significant changes.
They traded Mayank Dagar for Shahbaz Ahmed from RCB and made headlines by acquiring Pat Cummins for
Rs. 20.50 crore. Additional signings included Travis Head, Jaydev Unadkat, and Wanindu Hasaranga,
alongside investments in promising talents Akash Singh and Jhathavedh Subramanyan, demonstrating their commitment
to building a competitive squad.
